Customer Segment Action
1. Champions
Definition: Customers who purchased recently, frequently, and spend the most.
Business Actions:
* Provide exclusive VIP offers and early access to new products.
* Offer premium loyalty rewards and personalized discounts.
* Encourage referrals and customer testimonials.
* Upsell high-value or premium products.
2. Loyal Customers
Definition: Customers who purchase regularly and have good spending history.
Business Actions:
* Send personalized product recommendations.
* Offer loyalty programs and reward points.
* Provide subscription or membership plans.
* Engage them through email marketing and festive offers.
3. At Risk Customers
Definition: Customers who were frequent buyers but have not purchased recently.
Business Actions:
* Send win-back campaigns with special discounts.
* Offer limited-time promotions.
* Send reminder emails about previous purchases.
* Provide reactivation coupons.
4. Regular Customers
Definition: Customers with average purchase frequency and spending.
Business Actions:
* Send seasonal promotions and bundle offers.
* Encourage repeat purchases with small incentives.
* Recommend related products.
